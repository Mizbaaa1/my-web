Diwali

Divali also known as the festival of lights ,is one of the most important festivals celebrated in india and across the world .It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.During the festival,people decorate their homes with oil lamps,candles and colorful rangolis.Family exchange sweets,burst fair works ,and workship goddess lakshmi for prosperity and happiness.Diwali brings joy,togetherness and hope ,making it truely special occasion for everyone
